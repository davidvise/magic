Mule Magic

Proof that simple things can amaze...

".... we are going to illustrate to you the power of MuleSoft and its magical properties that is born from the humble webAPI. This app will call a webAPI that will attempt to guess a number you are thinking between 0 and 63 by asking you some simple questions. Go ahead and give it a try!!! ...."

You'll be shown a series of tables with numbers in them, you just need to click the 'YES' or 'NO' button in relation to your secret numbers existence in the table. The app will show you 6 tables and then amaze you by showing you your number.
